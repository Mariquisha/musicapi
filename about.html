About Us

Moody Music is an app that brings new music to you based on your Mood. Excited? Sad? Confused?

Music is often used as a vehicle to help express emotions that are difficult to talk about with people. Any mood or emotion you're feeling, Moody Music will deliver an awesome tune that will help you express those thoughts.
